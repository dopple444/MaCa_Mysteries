# Spoiler Review Checklist

Last updated: 2026-05-19

Use this checklist before publishing a first-party game version.

## Public Content

- Game title does not reveal victim, killer, motive, method, or solution.
- Tagline and description are spoiler-free.
- Player count, duration, themes, and product copy are safe for public browsing.
- Public character bios do not identify the victim or killer unless intended by design.

## Host-Safe Content

- Host party control does not casually show killer identity before final reveal.
- Host-safe summaries do not include final solution details.
- Host can manage guests, assignments, rounds, evidence, media, accusations, and reveal controls without seeing protected solution text by accident.

## Player-Private Content

- Player-private cards only appear to the assigned character.
- Private character background does not reveal the killer unless intentionally designed.
- Player-private evidence only appears to assigned players after the correct reveal or round state.
- Unassigned players do not receive character-private clues.

## Round Unlocks

- Round 1 contains pre-murder interaction content only.
- Round 2 can reveal the victim after the murder begins.
- Round 2 investigation clues do not reveal the killer outright unless intended.
- Round 3 opens accusations before the final solution.
- Final reveal is the first normal moment where killer identity and full solution become visible.

## Victim Reveal

- Victim character is not exposed in public catalog copy.
- Victim identity is not exposed to the victim’s player before the murder reveal unless the game intentionally requires it.
- Victim reveal text appears only after the host reveals victim or starts the correct reveal flow.

## Killer Reveal

- Killer character is not exposed in public or host-safe views before final reveal.
- Killer reveal text appears only after final solution reveal.
- Any exception is documented in the game design notes.

## Media And Evidence

- Media visibility matches the clue it supports.
- Evidence tied to later rounds does not appear early.
- Spoiler-protected evidence body text is not shown in player views.
- Uploaded media passes MIME type and size policy before storage.

## Publishing Gate

- At least one character exists.
- At least one round exists.
- Final reveal exists.
- Critical clues are mapped to the right round and visibility.
- Admin reviewer signs off before setting version to `PUBLISHED`.
